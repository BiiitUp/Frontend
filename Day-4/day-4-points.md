Forms:
    Form is used to collect information from the user.

    Syntax:
        <form>
            ...form elements
        </form>
    
    Form elements:
        1. Input - Text, Password, Email, Number, Date, File, Radio, Checkbox, Select, Textarea
        2. Button - Submit, Reset
        3. Label - Used to label form elements
    Attributes:
        1. value
        2. disabled
